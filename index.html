Shahmy Shaheed's Epic Coastal Walk

Digital creator Shahmy Shaheed has taken on and successfully completed one of Sri Lanka's toughest challenges: Walking around the entire country from Beruwala to Beruwala over the course of 45 days covering approximately 1,500 kilometers.

Cricwire

Shahmee Shaheed, a determined and adventurous young man, has etched his name in the annals of Sri Lankan history by completing an extraordinary feat. Shahmee embarked on a grueling journey, walking over 1,500 kilometers around the coastal area of Sri Lanka, starting and ending in Beruwala. This epic trek, which took him 45 days to complete, is a testament to his unwavering resolve and endurance.

Shahmee's journey was not just about covering distances; it was about experiencing the diverse landscapes, cultures, and people of Sri Lanka. Along the way, he passed through numerous towns and villages, from the bustling streets of Colombo to the serene beaches of Jaffna, encountering challenges that tested both his physical and mental limits. His journey was marked by moments of solitude, reflection, and deep connection with the land he traversed.

By completing this monumental task, Shahmee Shaheed has not only set a record but also inspired countless others to pursue their dreams with passion and perseverance. His walk is a reminder that with determination and grit, no challenge is too great to overcome.
